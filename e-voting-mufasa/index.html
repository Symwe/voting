  • Manifesto: Unity and Creativity for Margaret Trowell School Community

    Introduction: We, the students, alumni, and staff of Margaret Trowell School of Industrial and Fine Arts at Makerere University, unite under a common vision to foster creativity, collaboration, and community spirit within our esteemed institution. With a rich legacy of artistic excellence and cultural heritage, we pledge to uphold the values that define our school and propel it towards greater heights. 1. Promoting Unity: - Establish regular gatherings and events to facilitate interactions and camaraderie among students, alumni, and staff. - Create online platforms and forums for continuous engagement and networking opportunities. - Foster a culture of inclusivity and mutual respect, celebrating diversity in all its forms.

    2. Nurturing Creativity: - Provide access to state-of-the-art facilities and resources for artistic expression and experimentation. - Organize workshops, seminars, and masterclasses conducted by renowned artists to inspire and enhance creative skills. - Encourage interdisciplinary collaboration and innovation across various fields of art and design.

    3. Advocating for Excellence: - Strive for academic excellence by promoting rigorous curriculum standards and providing support for academic pursuits. - Recognize and celebrate outstanding achievements in art, design, and academia through awards and honors. - Foster mentorship programs where experienced alumni and faculty members guide and support students in their artistic journey.

    4. Engaging with the Community: - Initiate outreach programs to engage with local communities and address social issues through art and creativity. - Collaborate with external organizations and institutions to showcase the talent and contributions of Margaret Trowell School to the wider society. - Encourage service learning initiatives where students apply their artistic skills for the betterment of society.

    5. Preserving Heritage and Legacy: - Document and preserve the rich history and heritage of Margaret Trowell School through archival initiatives and exhibitions. - Engage with alumni to collect and share stories, memories, and experiences, thereby strengthening the bond with the school. - Uphold the values and traditions that have shaped Margaret Trowell School into a beacon of artistic excellence and cultural significance.
